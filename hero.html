FlexJobs recognizes Red Hat as one of the Top 100 Companies with Remote Jobs

FlexJobs has once again named Red Hat among the top companies for remote workers in 2015. This award recognizes companies who post the most jobs with remote work options and who have exceptional flexibility in geographic locations. Around 25% of Red Hat associates work remotely, and they contribute to our unique culture and work environment.
